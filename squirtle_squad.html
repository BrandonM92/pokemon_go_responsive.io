Code of Conduct:

  1. Respect and Civility:
    • Treat all members with respect and kindness.
    • Avoid offensive language, discrimination, and personal attacks.
  2. Stay On Topic:
    • Keep discussions related to Pokemon Go and club activities.
    • Avoid drifting into unrelated topics or spamming.
  3. No Cheating or Hacking:
    • Do not promote or discuss cheating methods, hacks, or exploits.
    • Play the game fairly and follow Niantic's terms of service.
  4. Privacy and Safety:
    • Do not share personal information or location details of yourself or others.
    • Be cautious when meeting other members in real life and prioritize safety.
  5. Content Guidelines:
    • Refrain from posting inappropriate or NSFW content.
    • Respect copyright laws when sharing images, videos, or other media.
  6. Constructive Criticism:
    • Provide feedback and suggestions in a constructive manner.
    • Avoid excessive negativity or criticism without offering solutions.
  7. No Advertising or Self-Promotion:
    • Do not promote personal businesses, products, or services unrelated to the club.
    • Advertising within the designated channels with moderator approval is permitted.
  8. Moderator Instructions:
    • Follow instructions from moderators and club administrators.
    • Contact moderators for assistance or report any issues promptly.
  9. Multiple Accounts:
    • Do not create or use multiple accounts to circumvent bans or rules.
    • Each member should have only one active account.
  10. Updates and Amendments:
    • Rules may be updated or amended by moderators as necessary.
    • Members will be informed of any changes through announcements or pinned posts.

By participating in the forum, you agree to abide by these rules and contribute positively to the Pokemon Go club community.
